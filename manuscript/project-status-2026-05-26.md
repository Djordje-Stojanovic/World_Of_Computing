## What The User Can Provide

The highest-leverage help is not routine research; Codex can keep doing that. The useful human inputs are judgment calls:

1. Choose the publication path: self-published first, traditional submission, or private ARC before public launch.
2. Choose the target reader: general business reader, technical founder/operator, AI practitioner, or prize/journalist audience.
3. Pick tolerance for visuals: all-rights-clean only, fair-use with careful notes, or minimal-risk chart/SVG-only edition.
4. Give taste feedback on 2-3 sample chapters: where the prose feels alive, where it feels too dense, and where it loses you.
5. Decide whether the book should be more industrial history, more product/business history, or more technical explanation when those instincts conflict.
