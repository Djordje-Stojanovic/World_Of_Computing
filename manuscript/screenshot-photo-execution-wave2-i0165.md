## Caption Rules

Product pages may show what the vendor put in public, but they do not prove adoption, revenue, market share, productivity, customer outcomes, ecosystem size, or present-day product behavior.

Source pages may show provenance and source-role texture, but they do not prove benchmark crowns, exact performance, live ranks, price-quality frontiers, autonomy, safety, or broad deployment.

Physical infrastructure photographs may show cooling, buildings, racks, substations, or facility scale, but they do not prove a named AI workload, GPU count, energy-per-token, PUE, fuel mix, deployment schedule, customer use, or solved power/cooling constraints.
