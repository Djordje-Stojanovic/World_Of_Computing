# Master 100-Exhibit Selection Matrix - I-0182

Pass I-0182 ranks the existing exhibit universe into a provisional top-100 program. It does not create new art, commit rasters, or clear rights. The goal is selection discipline: preserve chapter balance, reduce duplicate GTC/infrastructure visuals, and make the next rights/render work obvious.

## Result

- `data/master_100_exhibit_selection_matrix_i0182.tsv` selects 100 exhibit candidates across all 24 chapters.
- `data/master_100_exhibit_reserve_drop_i0182.tsv` records the main reserve/drop decisions, especially duplicate GTC slides, dense Chapter 16 source surfaces, and product/IDE screenshots that should not all survive.
- The selected program still includes private-use screenshots and photo candidates. Those rows are not publication-ready until raster hashes, exact licenses, attribution, rights/fair-use decisions, and final render QA exist.

## Selection Rules

The matrix favors one strong visual job per row: mechanism, source texture, product surface, physical texture, claim blocker, or synthesis. It deliberately prunes repeated diagrams and duplicate product/source surfaces where a chapter would otherwise become a visual pile.

The biggest unresolved work is not raw count. It is converting selected acquisition-ready screenshots/photos into verified, rights-reviewed, layout-tested exhibits.
