# I-0208 Rights-Safe Industrial Synthesis Substitutes

This pass turns the risky-photo results from I-0207 into original SVG alternatives. The goal is not to remove all photos from the book; it is to ensure that every high-risk industrial photo has a rights-safe fallback that can carry the same explanatory job without licensing ambiguity or accidental overclaiming.

## Produced Substitutes

- A-0156 replaces the unverified transmission-line photo path with a grid/interconnection timing schematic.
- A-0157 replaces the unverified gas-turbine photo path with a neutral speed-to-power response matrix.
- A-0158 replaces unresolved cooling/nuclear tower photo paths with a rack-to-heat-rejection loop.
- A-0159 fills the TSMC/HBM photo gap with an original advanced-packaging stack schematic.

## Promotion Rule

These SVGs should replace photos when the photo would do only atmospheric work, when exact license/attribution remains unresolved, or when a photograph would invite claims the evidence does not support. A photo can still win in final layout if it has exact rights, strong visual drama, and a caption that blocks capacity, allocation, delivered-power, workload, emissions, yield, and supplier-rank claims.

## Editorial Effect

The industrial chapter art now has a fallback ladder: use verified public-domain/CC0 photos for texture, use conditional CC BY photos only with exact attribution, and use these synthesis diagrams when the book needs mechanism more than spectacle.
