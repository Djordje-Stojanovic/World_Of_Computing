# Chapter 11 Supported-Vs-Gap Source Board

Status: promoted visual package, I-0147, 2026-05-26.

This pass executes the Chapter 11 visual direction from the I-0137 audit: the China/open-frontier chapter needs a source-permission board, not another family tree or leaderboard. The new visual is `A-0118`, `assets/visual_system/chapter11-supported-vs-gap-source-board.svg`.

## What The Figure Does

The left side shows primary-source-supported lanes: Qwen2/Qwen3, DeepSeek-V3/R1, GLM/Z.ai, and Kimi/Moonshot. The right side keeps support-pending gaps visible: Qwen 3.5/3.6, DeepSeek V4-era, and MiniMax/Baidu/Tencent/Xiaomi MiMo/StepFun.

The figure is intentionally not a logo parade, chronology, model ranking, or adoption map. It is a claim-control surface for Chapter 11.

## Promotion Rationale

This improves the champion because Chapter 11 can now show both what is sourced and what remains blocked without laundering missing evidence into silence. It also differentiates Chapter 11 from Chapter 10's open-weight control-stack visuals and prevents a premature benchmark or national-race chart.

## Remaining Blockers

Screenshot capture, Qwen 3.5/3.6 primary-source support, DeepSeek V4-era support, MiniMax/Baidu/Tencent/Xiaomi MiMo/StepFun source packs, exact benchmark extraction, adoption, deployment, price-quality, and final render placement remain blocked.
