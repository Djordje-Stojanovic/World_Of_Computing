# Chapter 1 Visual Package - I-0123

Status: promoted visual package, pass I-0123, 2026-05-26.

This pass adds three rights-safe SVG diagrams for Chapter 1:

- A-0068, `chapter1-chatgpt-shock-chronology.svg`: separates ChatGPT launch chronology, Altman's one-million-users post, and Reuters/UBS/Similarweb MAU/daily-unique estimates into distinct evidence lanes.
- A-0069, `chapter1-token-to-answer-schematic.svg`: explains the prompt-to-token-to-next-token loop without asserting exact tokenizer behavior or truth.
- A-0070, `chapter1-institutional-response-chronology.svg`: maps Stack Overflow, NYC schools, Seattle schools, and JPMorgan as named local institutional responses.

The package intentionally blocks fastest-growing-app narration, OpenAI-confirmed adoption totals, paid users, revenue, productivity, broad public panic, all-developer claims, national school-ban claims, exact tokenizer/vocabulary behavior, human-like understanding, guaranteed reasoning, and truthfulness claims.

Source and claim permissions are mirrored in `data/chapter1_shock_visual_package_i0123.tsv` and `assets_manifest.tsv`.
