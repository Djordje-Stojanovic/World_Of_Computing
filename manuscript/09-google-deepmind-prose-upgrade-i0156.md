# Chapter 9 Google/DeepMind Prose Upgrade - I-0156

Pass I-0156 strengthens Chapter 9 by making Google's problem one of product conversion rather than simple delay. The opening now frames Google as research leader, infrastructure owner, ad incumbent, mobile platform, document suite, cloud provider, and consumer habit machine at once. That makes the post-ChatGPT tension sharper: Google had ingredients, but the public interface arrived elsewhere first.

The pass also clarifies the TPU section. TPUs are presented as leverage, not triumph: they matter when they become answers, latency, price, privacy, reliability, and integration that users and developers can feel.

No new external factual claim is introduced. The pass preserves blockers around Gemini adoption, subscriber totals, benchmark superiority, revenue, productivity, cloud share, Search click behavior, ad economics, market leadership, and TPU-vs-GPU cost/performance claims.
