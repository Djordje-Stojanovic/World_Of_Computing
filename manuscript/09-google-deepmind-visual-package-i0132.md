## Promotion Rationale

The chapter already had a supported prose spine from pass I-0111. These visuals make the main argument faster to see: Google's problem was converting a broad technical and product estate into a coherent Gemini experience. Each figure carries source IDs, claim-row ties, and explicit blockers, so the visuals improve clarity without laundering Google product posts into neutral market evidence.

## Remaining Blockers

- Exact Gemini benchmark superiority or live-rank claims.
- Gemini adoption, subscriber, active-use, productivity, revenue, and cloud-share claims.
- AI Overviews/Search click, publisher, or ad-economics measurements.
- TPU-versus-GPU cost, performance, availability, or TCO comparisons.
- Claims that long context solved retrieval, memory, legal review, codebase understanding, or enterprise knowledge work.
- Exact cutoff-day model-card lists without row-normalized snapshot extraction.
