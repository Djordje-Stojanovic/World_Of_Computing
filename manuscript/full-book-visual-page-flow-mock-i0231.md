## Main Findings

The book is visually rich but not yet visually calm. Chapters 2 through 5 need relief because each has a single selected visual; Chapters 15 and 16 need pruning because GTC, infrastructure, source surfaces, and power imagery crowd the same part of the book; Chapters 17 and 21 through 24 risk ending the book as a run of diagrams and blocker grids. Chapter 7 and Chapter 16 need special handling so screenshot/photo/source-texture clusters do not become galleries.

## Claim Contract

Allowed:

- Use this pass to route layout, caption, rights, and replacement decisions.
- Use mock page bands as planning locations.
- Use pressure labels to prioritize page-flow and rights triage.

Blocked:

- Treating mock bands as final page numbers.
- Treating selected screenshot/photo slots as publication-ready.
- Increasing figure count merely to fill white space.
- Removing blocker captions to make pages prettier.

Promotion rationale: this pass makes whole-book visual rhythm inspectable. It helps the book feel luxurious and alive rather than stuffed, while preserving the truth gates that keep visual polish from becoming overclaiming.
